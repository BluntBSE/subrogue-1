All entities have an EntityMoveBus that has a queue on them. 
As part of their process function, Entities do something like:
    update_coords()
    move_to_next()  - Here's where the entity moves to the next item in the Bus queue, if any.
    check_reached_waypoint()

Move commands are generated with GlobeHelpers.generate_move_command(Entity, target_position)
MoveCommands are what the EntityBus queue contains.
